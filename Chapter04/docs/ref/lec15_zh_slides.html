回顾: \(L_1(\mathbb{R})\) 函数的傅里叶变换

Fourier transform and its inverse as principal values

\[ \begin{aligned} \mathcal{F}\{x\}(j \omega) & =\lim _{T \rightarrow \infty} \int_{-T}^{T} x(t) e^{-j \omega t} d t \\ \mathcal{F}^{-1}(X)(t) & =\lim _{W \rightarrow \infty} \frac{1}{2 \pi} \int_{-W}^{W} X(j \omega) e^{j \omega t} d \omega \end{aligned} \]

定理。 (Pointwise inversion)

  1. If \(x \in L_{1}(\mathbb{R})\) satisfies Dirichlet conditions on all finite intervals,

\[ \left(\mathcal{F}^{-1} \circ \mathcal{F}\{x\}\right)(t)=\frac{x\left(t_{+}\right)+x\left(t_{-}\right)}{2}, \quad \forall t \]

  1. If \(X \in L_{1}(\mathbb{R})\) satisfies Dirichlet conditions on all finite intervals,

\[ \left(\mathcal{F} \circ \mathcal{F}^{-1}\{X\}\right)(j \omega)=\frac{X\left(j \omega_{+}\right)+X\left(j \omega_{-}\right)}{2}, \quad \forall \omega \]

  1. If \(x \in C(\mathbb{R}) \cap L_{1}(\mathbb{R})\) and \(\mathcal{F}\{x\} \in L_{1}(\mathbb{R})\), then \(\mathcal{F}^{-1} \circ \mathcal{F}\{x\}=x\)

回顾: 广义函数的傅里叶变换

施瓦茨空间 \(\mathcal{S}=\mathcal{S}(\mathbb{R})\) of rapidly decreasing functions

\[ \mathcal{S}=\left\{\phi \in C^{\infty}(\mathbb{R}):\|\phi\|_{\ell, k}<\infty, \forall \ell, k \in \mathbb{N}\right\} \]

where \(\|\phi\|_{\ell, k}=\sup _{t \in \mathbb{R}}\left|t^{\ell} \phi^{(k)}(t)\right|\). Note \(\mathcal{F}\{\mathcal{S}\}=\mathcal{S}\). Fourier transform of tempered distributions

\[ (x, \phi) \triangleq \int_{\mathbb{R}} x(\xi) \phi(\xi) d \xi \]

  • If \(x_{n} \rightarrow x\) in distribution, then \(\mathcal{F}\{x\} \triangleq \lim _{n} \mathcal{F}\left\{x_{n}\right\}\) in distribution

\[ (x, \phi)=\lim _{n}\left(x_{n}, \phi\right) \Longrightarrow(\mathcal{F}\{x\}, \phi) \triangleq \lim _{n}\left(\mathcal{F}\left\{x_{n}\right\}, \phi\right), \quad \forall \phi \in \mathcal{S} \]

  • Alternatively, \((\mathcal{F}\{x\}, \phi) \triangleq(x, \mathcal{F}\{\phi\}), \quad \forall \phi \in \mathcal{S}\)

回顾: Fourier Transform for Periodic Functions

傅里叶级数

\[ x(t)=\sum_{k=-\infty}^{\infty} \hat{x}[k] e^{j k \omega_{0} t} \]

Fourier transform

\[ X(j \omega)=\sum_{k=-\infty}^{\infty} 2 \pi \hat{x}[k] \delta\left(\omega-k \omega_{0}\right) \]

等价表示

  • \(\hat{x}[k]\) is amplitude of component at \(\omega_{k}=k \omega_{0}\)
  • \(\hat{x}[k] \delta\left(\omega-k \omega_{0}\right)\) is density of component at \(\omega_{k}=k \omega_{0}\)

Cf. discrete random variable \(x\) in probability

\[ \mathbb{E} f(X)=\sum_{n} f\left(x_{n}\right) p_{n}=\int_{\mathbb{R}} f(x) p(x) d x \text { with } p(x)=\sum_{n} p_{n} \delta\left(x-x_{n}\right) \]

回顾: 傅里叶级数与傅里叶变换的关系

  • Aperiodic signal \(x\) with finite support of length \(<T\)
  • Periodic extension \(x_{T}\) of \(x\)

\[ x_{T}(t)=\sum_{k=-\infty}^{\infty} x(t-k T) \]

NB. In other words, \(x\) is one period of periodic signal \(x_{T}\)

\[ \begin{array}{rlr} x \stackrel{\mathcal{F}}{\longleftrightarrow} X & \hat{x}_{T}[k]=\frac{1}{T} X\left(j k \omega_{0}\right) \\ x_{T} \stackrel{\mathcal{F S}}{\longleftrightarrow} \hat{x}_{T} & X_{T}(j \omega)=\sum_{k=-\infty}^{\infty} 2 \pi \hat{x}_{T}[k] \delta\left(\omega-k \omega_{0}\right) \\ x_{T} \stackrel{\mathcal{F}}{\longleftrightarrow} X_{T} & =\sum_{k=-\infty}^{\infty} \omega_{0} X\left(j k \omega_{0}\right) \delta\left(\omega-k \omega_{0}\right) \end{array} \]
