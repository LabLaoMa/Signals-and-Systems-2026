DTFT 的卷积性质

\[ (x * h)[n] \stackrel{\mathcal{F}}{\longleftrightarrow} X\left(e^{j \omega}\right) H\left(e^{j \omega}\right) \]

NB. Dual 的 multiplication property 的 CTFS

\[ x(t) y(t) \stackrel{\mathcal{F S}}{\longleftrightarrow}(\hat{x} * \hat{y})[k] \]

NB. Similar 到 CTFT, applicable when 用于mula 是 我们ll-defined Pro的.

\[ \begin{aligned} \mathcal{F}\{x * h\}\left(e^{j \omega}\right) & =\sum_{n \in \mathbb{Z}}(x * h)[n] e^{-j \omega n}=\sum_{n \in \mathbb{Z}} \sum_{m \in \mathbb{Z}} x[m] h[n-m] e^{-j \omega n} \\ & =\sum_{m \in \mathbb{Z}} x[m] \sum_{n \in \mathbb{Z}} h[n-m] e^{-j \omega n} \\ & =\sum_{m \in \mathbb{Z}} x[m] e^{-j \omega m} H\left(e^{j \omega}\right) \\ & =X\left(e^{j \omega}\right) H\left(e^{j \omega}\right) \end{aligned} \]

LTI 系统的频率响应

LTI 系统 \(T\)

  • fully characterized by impulse 响应 \(h\)

\[ y=T(x)=h * x \]

  • also fully characterized by frequency 响应 \(H=\mathcal{F}\{h\}\), if \(H\) 是 我们ll-defined
  • BIBO stable 系统, \(h \in \ell_{1}\)
  • other 系统s: accumula到r \(h=u\), …

Typically, convolution property implies

\[ Y=\mathcal{F}\{y\}=H X=\mathcal{F}\{h\} \mathcal{F}\{x\} \]

Instead 的 computing \(x * h\), can do

\[ y=\mathcal{F}^{-1}(\mathcal{F}\{h\} \mathcal{F}\{x\}) \]

例

Response 的 LTI 系统 with impulse 响应 \(h[n]=a^{n} u[n]\) 到 input \(x[n]=b^{n} u[n]\), where \(|a|<1,|b|<1\) 方法 1. Direct convolution \(y=x * h\) 方法 2. Solve difference equation with initial rest condition

\[ y[n]-a y[n-1]=b^{n} u[n] \]

方法 3. Fourier trans用于m.

\[ H=\frac{1}{1-a e^{-j \omega}}, X=\frac{1}{1-b e^{-j \omega}} \Longrightarrow Y=\frac{1}{\left(1-a e^{-j \omega}\right)\left(1-b e^{-j \omega}\right)} \]

If \(a \neq b, Y\left(e^{j \omega}\right)=\frac{a /(a-b)}{1-a e^{-j \omega}}-\frac{b /(a-b)}{1-b e^{-j \omega}}, \quad y[n]=\frac{1}{a-b}\left(a^{n+1}-b^{n+1}\right) u[n]\) If \(a=b, Y\left(e^{j \omega}\right)=\frac{d}{d a}\left(\frac{a}{1-a e^{-j \omega}}\right), y[n]=\frac{d}{d a} a^{n+1} u[n]=(n+1) a^{n} u[n]\) Or use \(Y\left(e^{j \omega}\right)=\frac{j}{a} e^{j \omega} \frac{d}{d \omega}\left(\frac{a}{1-a e^{-j \omega}}\right)\) 与 differentiation property

DTFT 的乘积性质

\[ x[n] y[n] \stackrel{\mathcal{F}}{\longleftrightarrow} \frac{1}{2 \pi} X \circledast Y=\frac{1}{2 \pi} \int_{2 \pi} X\left(e^{j \theta}\right) Y\left(e^{j(\omega-\theta)}\right) d \theta \]

NB. Dual 的 periodic convolution property 的 CTFS

\[ x \circledast y \stackrel{\mathcal{F S}}{\longleftrightarrow} T \hat{x} \hat{y} \]

Pro的.

\[ \begin{aligned} \mathcal{F}\{x y\}\left(e^{j \omega}\right) & =\sum_{n \in \mathbb{Z}} x[n] y[n] e^{-j \omega n}=\sum_{n \in \mathbb{Z}}\left(\frac{1}{2 \pi} \int_{2 \pi} X\left(e^{j \theta}\right) e^{j \theta n} d \theta\right) y[n] e^{-j \omega n} \\ & =\frac{1}{2 \pi} \int_{2 \pi} X\left(e^{j \theta}\right)\left(\sum_{n \in \mathbb{Z}} y[n] e^{-j(\omega-\theta) n}\right) d \theta \\ & =\frac{1}{2 \pi} \int_{2 \pi} X\left(e^{j \theta}\right) Y\left(e^{j(\omega-\theta)}\right) d \theta \end{aligned} \]

Linear Constant-coefficient Difference Equations

Frequency 响应 的 LTI 系统 described by

\[ \sum_{k=0}^{N} a_{k} y[n-k]=\sum_{k=0}^{M} b_{k} x[n-k] \]

方法 1. Use eigen函数 property

\[ x[n]=e^{j \omega n} \Longrightarrow y[n]=H\left(e^{j \omega}\right) e^{j \omega n} \]

Substitution in到 difference equation yields

\[ \begin{gathered} \sum_{k=0}^{N} a_{k} H\left(e^{j \omega}\right) e^{j \omega(n-k)}=\sum_{k=0}^{M} b_{k} e^{j \omega(n-k)} \\ H\left(e^{j \omega}\right)=\frac{\sum_{k=0}^{M} b_{k} e^{-j \omega k}}{\sum_{k=0}^{N} a_{k} e^{-j \omega k}} \end{gathered} \]

Linear Constant-coefficient Difference Equations

方法 2. Take Fourier trans用于m 的 both sides

\[ \mathcal{F}\left\{\sum_{k=0}^{N} a_{k} y[n-k]\right\}=\mathcal{F}\left\{\sum_{k=0}^{M} b_{k} x[n-k]\right\} \]

By linearity 与 time-shifting property

\[ \sum_{k=0}^{N} a_{k} e^{-j \omega k} Y\left(e^{j \omega}\right)=\sum_{k=0}^{M} b_{k} e^{-j \omega k} X\left(e^{j \omega}\right) \]

By convolution property

\[ H\left(e^{j \omega}\right)=\frac{Y\left(e^{j \omega}\right)}{X\left(e^{j \omega}\right)}=\frac{\sum_{k=0}^{M} b_{k} e^{-j \omega k}}{\sum_{k=0}^{N} a_{k} e^{-j \omega k}} \]

Frequency 响应 是 rational 函数 的 \(e^{-j \omega}\)

例

\[ h[n]=4\left(\frac{1}{2}\right)^{n} u[n]-2\left(\frac{1}{4}\right)^{n} u[n] \]

逆傅里叶变换 的 \(\frac{1}{\left(1-a e^{-j \omega}\right)^{n}}\)

Recall

\[ a^{n} u[n] \stackrel{\mathcal{F}}{\longleftrightarrow} \frac{1}{1-a e^{-j \omega}}, \quad|a|<1 \]

i.e.

\[ \frac{1}{1-a z}=\sum_{n=0}^{\infty} a^{n} z^{n}, \quad z=e^{-j \omega} \]

Differentiate \(m-1\) times w.r.t. \(z\)

\[ \frac{(m-1)!a^{m-1}}{(1-a z)^{m}}=\sum_{n=m-1}^{\infty} a^{n} \frac{n!}{(n-m+1)!} z^{n-(m-1)} \]

\[ \frac{1}{(1-a z)^{m}}=\sum_{n=m-1}^{\infty} a^{n-m+1}\binom{n}{m-1} z^{n-m+1}=\sum_{n=0}^{\infty} a^{n}\binom{n+m-1}{m-1} z^{n} \]

Thus

\[ \binom{n+m-1}{n} a^{n} u[n] \stackrel{\mathcal{F}}{\longleftrightarrow} \frac{1}{\left(1-a e^{-j \omega}\right)^{m}} \]

离散时间采样定理

B与-limited DT signal \(x[n]\) whose spectrum \(X\left(e^{j \omega}\right)=0\) 用于 \(\omega_{M}<|\omega| \leq \pi\) 是 uniquely determined by its samples \(x[k N], k \in \mathbb{Z}\) if

\[ \omega_{s} \triangleq \frac{2 \pi}{N}>2 \omega_{M} \]

Given \(\{x[k N]: k \in \mathbb{Z}\}, x[n]\) can be reconstructed as follows

  1. construct \(x_{p}[n]=\sum_{k=-\infty}^{\infty} x[k N] \delta[n-k N]\)
  2. send \(x_{p}\) through lowpass filter with gain \(N\) 与 cut的f frequency \(\omega_{c} \in\left(\omega_{M}, \omega_{s}-\omega_{M}\right)\), i.e.

\[ H\left(e^{j \omega}\right)=N\left[u\left(\omega+\omega_{c}\right)-u\left(\omega-\omega_{c}\right)\right] \]

  1. output \(x_{r}[n]\) with \(X_{r}\left(e^{j \omega}\right)=X_{p}\left(e^{j \omega}\right) H\left(e^{j \omega}\right)\) 是 same as \(x[n]\)

时域重建

Impulse 响应 的 lowpass filter

\[ h[n]=\frac{N \sin \left(\omega_{c} n\right)}{\pi n} \]

\(x\) recovered by b与-limited interpolation using sinc 函数

\[ x[n]=x_{r}[n]=\left(x_{p} * h\right)[n]=\sum_{k=-\infty}^{\infty} x[k N] \frac{N \sin \left(\omega_{c}(n-k N)\right)}{\pi(n-k N)} \]
